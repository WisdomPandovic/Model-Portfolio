About Me

Chisom Okeke is a Nigerian model and influencer, born in Lagos, with Igbo roots.

She is one of the most successful models from Africa, conquering the international fashion market in Europe, Asia and America. Chisom has had a dazzling career, being published in Elle, Vogue, Tatler, L'officiel, Nylon and Harper's Bazaar.

Besides that, she's always been passionate about fashion and modeling ever since she was little.

My Story

Chisom Okeke, a Lagos-born model of Igbo heritage, discovered her profound love for fashion and modeling in the bustling streets of Lagos. Growing up in this vibrant city, Chisom was immersed in a rich cultural tapestry that ignited her passion for self-expression through clothing and style.

Chisom's journey into the world of fashion began when she attended a local fashion show, mesmerized by the runway, the elegant garments, and the confident models. Inspired by the creativity and diversity on display, she found herself drawn to the transformative power of fashion.

Embracing her Igbo roots, Chisom recognized the unique blend of tradition and modernity that could be expressed through clothing. This realization fueled her desire to become a model who could seamlessly integrate cultural heritage with contemporary fashion trends.

Driven by this newfound passion, Chisom embarked on her modeling journey, participating in local events and collaborating with emerging designers. Her distinctive style, influenced by the vibrant colors and patterns of Igbo culture, set her apart in the competitive world of fashion.
